Events

Business Networking Mixer:

Join fellow entrepreneurs, business owners, and professionals for an evening of networking and collaboration. Exchange ideas, build meaningful connections, and explore potential partnerships to fuel your business growth in the vibrant economic landscape of Merida.

Economic Outlook Seminar:

Gain valuable insights into the economic trends and projections shaping the business landscape in Yucatan. Esteemed economists and industry experts will provide analysis and forecasts, equipping attendees with essential knowledge to make informed decisions and navigate the evolving marketplace effectively.

Entrepreneurship Workshop Series:

Empower yourself with practical skills and knowledge essential for entrepreneurial success. This workshop series covers a range of topics including business planning, marketing strategies, financial management, and innovation. Whether you're launching a startup or scaling your business, these sessions offer valuable guidance and resources tailored to your needs.

International Trade Forum:

Explore opportunities for global expansion and trade partnerships at this forum dedicated to international commerce. Connect with industry leaders, trade representatives, and government officials to discuss trade policies, market trends, and investment opportunities. Whether you're seeking to export your products or expand your supply chain, this event provides a platform to explore and facilitate international trade connections.

Tech Innovation Summit:

Dive into the cutting-edge world of technology and innovation at this summit designed to showcase the latest trends and advancements. From AI and blockchain to cybersecurity and digital transformation, discover how technology is revolutionizing industries and driving economic growth in Merida and beyond. Engage with tech pioneers, participate in interactive sessions, and explore opportunities to leverage technology for business success.
